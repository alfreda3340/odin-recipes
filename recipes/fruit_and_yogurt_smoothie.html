Fruit And Yogurt Smoothie

Yummy strawberry smoothie

This recipe is perfect for berry lovers with the ability to add whichever berries you love, the taste will be great regardless of how you mix and match.

  • 1 cup of berries of your choice
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • ¼ cup pineapple juice
  • 1 ½ teasppons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk
