Chocolate Smoothie

For the chocolate lovers

Been craving chocolate? this recipe will for sure satisfy that craving.

  • 2 cups unsweetend almond milk
  • ¼ cup quick oats
  • 5 prunes
  • 2 ripe frozen banana
  • ½ cup greek yogurt plain
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed meal
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
